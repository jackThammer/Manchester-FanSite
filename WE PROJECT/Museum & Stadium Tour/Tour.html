FOOTBALL STORY EVER TOLD

The story of Manchester United is unlike any other club in the world. Beginning more than a century ago,
it combines eras of total English and European domination with some of the greatest adversity
faced by any football club.

Only at the Old Trafford Museum & Tour Centre can you see the stadium through the eyes of
Manchester United greats themselves. Or get even closer to the world of Manchester United and share 130 years of football in the award-winning Museum.

Whether you’re a Manchester United fan or not, a visit to the Museum & Tour Centre is a day to remember!
