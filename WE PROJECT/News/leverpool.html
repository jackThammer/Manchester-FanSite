Rooney starts and skippers United, Suarez plays for Liverpool

25 Sep 2013 18:59

Manchester United manager David Moyes rung the changes against Liverpool in the Capital One Cup clash, with Luis Suarez starting for the visitors.

Manchester United manager David Moyes rung the changes against Liverpool, for whom Luis Suarez starts.

Defenders Rafael and Jonny Evans and Alexander Buttner were brought in, while the midfield was totally changed from Sunday's derby with Nani, Ryan Giggs, Phil Jones and Shinji Kagawa all in.

Up front Wayne Rooney took the captain's armband while Javier Hernandez also starts.

Liverpool boss Brendan Rodgers selected a strong side including controversial striker Luis Suarez, who returns after a 10-match ban for biting.

Teams

Man Utd: De Gea, Rafael Da Silva, Smalling, Evans, Buttner, Jones, Giggs, Nani, Rooney, Kagawa, Hernandez. Subs: Anderson, Carrick, Welbeck, Fabio Da Silva, Zaha, Amos, Januzaj.

Liverpool: Mignolet, Toure, Skrtel, Sakho, Jose Enrique, Gerrard, Lucas, Henderson, Suarez, Moses, Sturridge. Subs: Brad Jones, Alberto, Aspas, Sterling, Ibe, Kelly, Wisdom.
